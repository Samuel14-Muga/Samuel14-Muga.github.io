Projects

This is a section that contains all of the projects that i undertook at Gearbox Kenya and any other future projects that I might do. They involve the use of different micro-controllers such as the NodeMCU and the Arduino, different sensors such as the Soil Moisture Sensor, DHT11 Temperature and Humidity Sensor and Big Sound Sensor, and actuators such as the DC Motor. The repositories in this section contain documentations, flowcharts, fritzing files and videos of the projects.

Project 1: Smart Farm

This project aims at coming up with a system that can detect the moisture content in the soil. It pumps water to the soil when the moisture level falls below a certain level, and stops the pumping of water when it goes above a certain level.

Important components used in this project are:

  1. Arduino Mega
  2. Amphibious submerssible water pump
  3. Soil Moisure sensor

Project 2: Automated Fire Extinguisher

This project is a scale-down of a system that can be used to extinguish fires be it in a house or in large organisations. Upon the detection of a flame or extreme heat, a fan is turned on and sucks air from in and around the fire, thus extingushing it.

The important components used include:

  1. Arduino Mega
  2. L9110 Fan Module

Project 3: Home Automation

This project aims to automate important systems in the house, and in this case, lighting and temperature regulation. In temperature regulation, when the temperature around the house goes above a preset temperature, say 30 degrees Celcius, a fan turns on, cools the room till it falls to or below the preset temperature. In the lighting system, the switching on and off of the bulbs around the huse can be controlled by certain gestures, such as clapping of hands.

The important components used in the lighting system include:

  1. Arduino Mega
  2. Big Sound Sensor
  3. LED bulb

The important components used in the temperature regulation system include:

  1. Arduino Mega
  2. DHT11 Temperature and Humidity Sensor
  3. DC Motor

Project 5: Automated Door Lock

This project aims at controlling the locking and unlocking of a door through automation. Through the use of an authorized keycard, a person can be able to gain automatic access to a building. The door remains locked in the presence of an unauthourized keycard, or a lack thereof.

The important components used include:

  1. Esp 8266 NodeMCU
  2. RFID
  3. Servo Motors
  4. LED bulbs
